Why We Are Better

Open to all genders and sexualities

  • Genders on our site are crowdsourced. If none of them describe you, you can enter your own.

Our system encourages better and fewer introductory messages

  • Mutual interest hints encourage starting the conversation out right.
  • The dynamics of a typical dating site tends to turn men into spammers sending out hundreds of copy and paste messages in hopes of getting any reply. This just sucks for everyone. We limit this behavior with our Message Credit System.

Message Credits

Everyday each user receives a number of message credits. Sending a message to a user you have not previously received a message from will cost you a number of credits based on how likely they are to respond, so the more they are likely they are to like you the more messages you get. Replying to another user is always free. Message credits reset everyday.
